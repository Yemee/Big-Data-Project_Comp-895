##Getting Twitter API Keys##

For you to acces the Twitter Streaming API, the following steps should be implemented.

1). Create a twitter account if you do not have one yet.
2). Go to http://apps.twitter.com and log in with your twitter logins
3). Click " Create New App"
4). Fill out the form provided, agree to the terms and click on "Create your Twitter Application".
5). In the next page, click on "API Keys" tab and copy your "API Key" and "API Secret".
6). Finally scroll down and click "Create my access token", and copy your "Access token" and "Acess token secret"
